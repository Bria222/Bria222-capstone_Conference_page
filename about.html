Events254 Arena in collaboration with KICC

The Kenyatta International Convention Centre, formerly the Kenyatta International Conference Centre, is a 28-story building located in Nairobi, Kenya. The KICC is located in the City Square of Nairobi, and is a crucial address for a number of Government offices, including those of recently elected Senators The Kenyatta International Convention Centre is an icon and landmark for Kenya. KICC boasts of being the leading facility in the meetings industry in East Africa and has held quite a number of regional and international conferences like the World Bank Conference in 1973, Inter-parliamentary Union summit in 2008, other UN based conferences, 8th African Growth and Opportunity Act (AGOA), 7th Global Conference on health, the 5th Pan African Malaria conference, Internet Corporation for Assigned Names and Numbers (ICANN) Congress & the 2010 International Federation of Biomedical Laboratory Scientists (IFBLS), 10th WTO Meeting, TICAD VI, 2nd High Level Meeting on GPEDC among others.
